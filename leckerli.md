# Leckerli

## Ingredients

### Dough

| Name                | Amount  | Prep         |
| ------------------- | ------- | ------------ |
| honey               | 1 cup   |              |
| granulated sugar    | 1/2 cup |              |
| cinnamon            | 1/4 tsp | ground       |
| black pepper        | 1/4 tsp | ground       |
| white pepper        | 1/8 tsp | ground       |
| nutmeg              | 1/8 tsp | ground       |
| ginger              | 1/8 tsp | ground       |
| cloves              | 1/8 tsp | ground       |
| candied orange rind | 1/2 cup | finely diced |
| candied lemon rind  | 1/2 cup | finely diced |
| bourbon             | 1/4 cup |              |
| all-purpose flour   | 2 cups  |              |
| baking soda         | 1/2 tsp |              |

### Glaze

| Name                 | Amount |
| -------------------- | ------ |
| confectioners' sugar | 1 cup  |
| gin                  | 1 tbsp |
| lemon juice          | 1 tbsp |

## Recipe

### Dough

- To a saucepan, add honey and sugar
- Apply high heat until sugar fulls dissolves to form an amber syrup
and spices
- Turn off heat and add spices to honey syrup, mix thoroughly
- Into large mixing bowl, add honey syrup, candied citrus, and bourbon
- In a second bowl, mix flour and baking soda
- Add flour mix to honey syrup and mix thoroughly to form a wet dough, almost a batter
- Pour out dough onto a parchment-lined baking sheet
- Place in freezer until firm enough to roll
- Lightly flour and roll to 1/4 inch
- Bake at 350 for 20 minutes, rotating half way through
- Brush on glaze immediately after removing from the oven
- After cooling, cut into small rectangles (suggested 2x2 inches)

### Glaze

- In a bowl, mix bourbon, lemon juice, and confectioner's sugar to form a slurry
- Mix again directly before use

## Notes

These cookies will get chewier over time. Don't be afraid to make them a few days early and let them sit on the counter top.
