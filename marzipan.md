# Ingredients

| Name                       | Amount  |
|----------------------------|---------|
| fine blanched almond flour | 2 cups  |
| powdered sugar             | 1 cup   |
| water                      | 2 tbsp  |
| almond extract             | 2 tbsp  |
| rose water                 | 1/4 tsp |

# Recipe

1. Mix almond flour and powdered sugar.
1. Add water, almond extract, and rose water to dry ingredients.
1. Knead until uniform.
